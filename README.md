**Superstore Performance Dashboard (2017)**

This project is a **Tableau dashboard** that analyzes the performance of a Superstore dataset for the year **2017**.
The dashboard is designed to provide insights into **sales, profit, and overall business performance** through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard consists of three pages;
- Overview
- Sales
- Profit

**Overview**
- Key KPIs such as Total Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers and Profit Ratio
- Performance trends over time between 2016 and 2017
- Top performing customers
 
**Sales**
- Sales analysis by category, sub-category, region, and segment
- Monthly sales trends
- Identification of top-performing regions and state

**Profit**
- Profit distribution across categories and segments
- Identification of profitable and unprofitable categories
- Profit trends over time


**Tools Used**
- Tableau Desktop
- Microsoft Excel


**Dataset**
- Superstore Dataset

**Key Insights**
- Some sub-categories generated high sales but low or negative profit
- California state residents buy more of office supplies compared to other state residents who buy more of technology
- Furniture brings little to no profit even with high sales
- Some months had significantly high sales but did not contribute to profit
